Welcome to LifeFlow

The task manager that lives on your machine.
Fast, local, and yours.

3-step setup takes 2 minutes:
1️⃣ Create a life area (e.g., "Work", "Health")
2️⃣ Add a goal to that area
3️⃣ Create your first task

Step 1: Create a Life Area

Areas organize your life into categories. Example: Work, Health, Finances

Step 2: Add a Goal

Goals are what you want to achieve. Example: Complete Q1 project, Run 3x per week

Step 3: Create Your First Task

Tasks are concrete actions. Example: Email client proposal, Buy groceries
